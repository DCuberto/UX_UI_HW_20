D-Way Travel APP

My travel app was designed to achieve a user friendly way for people to book, edit, and plan trips. I have observed that different products or services weren’t providing an easy way for people to easily book and plan trips and change booking information without consequence, which is causing potential users to use another service. The goal was to create a travel app that allows the convenience of easily planning a trip and picking possible outings and base success off of user feedback.

Jonathan Smaine was the proto persona that acted as the basis for what we thought our userbase valued.

After the user interviews we more accurately came up with a possible empathy map for our userbase finding that people valued reliability and pricing.
